# User Journey

## 1. Pick Up the Handset
The user interacts with the device exactly like a traditional landline telephone. As soon as the handset is lifted, the Raspberry Pi detects the hook switch and opens a voice session automatically, without requiring any screen, button, or menu.

## 2. Talk About Life Memories
The AI opens the conversation and asks gentle questions about the user's life memories. It progressively guides the exchange from broad topics toward more specific anecdotes, helping the person share meaningful stories in a natural spoken format.

## 3. Transcription and Story Creation
During and after the conversation, the server processes the audio locally. Speech is transcribed, transformed into a more readable narrative story, and enriched with a title and estimated year for placement in the archive.

## 4. Family Timeline Access
The resulting story is saved into a chronological timeline accessible through a web application. Family members can browse memories, open each story in full, and revisit a growing archive of personal life narratives.
